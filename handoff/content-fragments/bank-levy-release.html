Lift a Freeze on Your Bank Account Before Funds Are Seized

When the IRS is owed unpaid tax debt, they have the authority to issue a bank levy that freezes your account and seize funds after 21 days to pay back your outstanding taxes. This can cause severe financial hardship on a taxpayer. Fortunately, there are ways to release a bank levy and prevent it from happening in the future. Priority Tax Relief acts quickly to contact the IRS, demonstrate your situation, and work toward a release, so you can regain funds before the 21-day window closes.

Bank Levy Release Services

Emergency IRS Contact

Our team works fast within the 21-day window between a frozen account and seized funds to contact the IRS and push for a release before the deadline.

Levy Error Review

We review bank levies to ensure there are no procedural errors, missed notice requirements, or improper application that could be grounds for release.

Financial Hardship Documentation

We prepare and submit documentation needed to qualify for a hardship-based levy release if you cannot cover basic living expenses without those funds.

Bank Coordination

Our team coordinates directly with your bank to confirm that the IRS hold is lifted and your account is fully restored after the IRS issues a levy release.

Ongoing Levy Protection

We help you address any underlying tax debt to strategize a tax resolution plan so the IRS cannot issue new levies to your accounts.

IRS Transcript Review

We pull IRS transcripts to verify that the levy was issued correctly, confirm funds, and identify discrepancies that could work in for you in negotiations.

How Long Does It Take to Release a Bank Levy?

Even the 21-day period between the IRS freezing a bank account and seizing the funds can be stressful, as those funds are inaccessible during that time. When our team successfully gets a bank levy release, the IRS typically lifts the hold on the account within one to two business days. After this happens, our team will work with you to ensure that the issue is fully resolved and you have access to your entire account balance within that frozen account.
