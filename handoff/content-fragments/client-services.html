Your case, step by step

What to expect once you're a client

Every case is different, but the path is the same. Here's how we move a tax matter from the day we open it through to the day it's resolved — and how to reach our client services team whenever you want a real-time update.

The resolution process

A typical case, start to finish

Six stages take most cases from intake to closed, and most run 6–12 months end to end. The ranges below are typical — your actual timeline depends on case complexity and how quickly the IRS responds.

  1. 1
    Stage 1

    Initial Case Prep

    We open your case and file IRS authorization to act on your behalf.

    Typical: 1–2 weeks
  2. 2
    Stage 2

    Case Analysis

    We pull your IRS transcripts and review your full financial picture.

    Typical: 2–4 weeks
  3. 3
    Stage 3

    Documentation

    We gather supporting documents and prepare the required forms.

    Typical: 3–6 weeks
  4. 4
    Stage 4

    Resolution

    We negotiate and present your most beneficial resolution to the IRS.

    Typical: 2–4 months
  5. 5
    Stage 5

    Case Finalization

    The IRS reviews and approves; we confirm the final terms with you.

    Typical: 2–6 weeks
  6. Stage 6

    Case Closed

    Your resolution is in place and we confirm everything is complete.

    Resolved

Timelines shown are typical estimates for planning purposes only and are not a guarantee. IRS processing times, the complexity of your case, and how quickly requested documents are returned all affect how long each stage takes.
